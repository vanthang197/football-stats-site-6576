Football Statistics Guide

This platform provides advanced football statistics to evaluate player performance. The data is inspired by professional analytics platforms such as LaLiga Advanced Stats, Opta, StatsBomb and Fantasy Premier League.

How to Read the Statistics

Metric Description Formula
M Minutes played Total minutes played by the player
G Goals scored Total goals scored
A Assists Final pass leading directly to a goal
S Shots Total shots attempted
SOT Shots on target Shots that would enter goal if not saved
KP Key passes Pass leading to a shot
T Tackles Successful defensive tackles
INT Interceptions Passes intercepted
YC Yellow cards Total yellow cards received
RC Red cards Total red cards received
G/90 Goals per 90 Goals ÷ Minutes × 90
A/90 Assists per 90 Assists ÷ Minutes × 90
S/90 Shots per 90 Shots ÷ Minutes × 90
